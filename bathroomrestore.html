    A FUNCTIONAL OASIS – THE BENEFITS OF A BATHROOM REMODEL

     

    As with your kitchen, the bathroom is one of the most frequented rooms of the home, you will get to experience the effects of your completely personalized remodel on a daily basis. However, unlike a kitchen remodel, if you have more than one bathroom in your home, opting to renovate will be less disruptive to your daily routine. As well, a bathroom remodel lets you not only to maximize your space by designing the perfect layout including additional storage, but also to install energy-efficient fixtures and water saving showers to help save both money and resources. Furthermore, should you decide to sell in the future, a bathroom remodel will potentially increase your home’s overall value and is a feature many buyers look for.

    A Seamless Bathroom Remodel

    Your average bathroom remodel may conjure up images of dust, debris and clutters, and months of sidestepping the chaos may seem like an eternity. At Tabor Design Build, we specialize in making the process at stress-free as possible. We ensure that the bathroom remodel is completed as quickly and smoothly as possible, with minimal disruption to your daily routine. A written project schedule will be provided before work even begins, and our tight scheduling process allows us to complete jobs much faster than typical industry averages.
