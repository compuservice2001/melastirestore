    Home Renovation

    The Value of a Melasti Home Renovation

     

    Home renovation is an excellent way to transform your existing space into an entirely new experience – saving you the cost of buying a new house or building from the ground up. With Tabor, you’ll benefit from decades of experience and a proven design+build process. We help you transform your space by introducing new elements, architectural features, luxury amenities, or personal touches. Most especially, we work with you in mind; helping you create the home you’ve always wanted.

    When It’s Not Perfect, Consider an Interior Renovation

    Let’s make it better. That’s what we’re all about at Tabor: a better way to create better spaces. If you’re tired of looking at the same old kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, or basement, let Tabor Design Build help. We guide you through each step of the process – from design to build and clean up – transforming your home’s interior from dull and drab to vibrant and vivacious. Upgrading your existing interior will not only add great visual appeal to your home, but it may also reduce maintenance and utility costs. Most renovations, even ones as simple as replacing windows or adding certain types of blinds, can add significant overall value to your home should you decide to sell in the future.

     

    Go All the Way with a Whole Home Renovation

    If you’re looking for a more dramatic transformation, consider renovating your entire home. We’ll work within the existing structure of your house to give you a completely new experience by reconfiguring the floor plan and updating the finishes, fixtures, and built-in amenities. We can remove walls and expand areas for an open floor plan, create better entertaining spaces or more relaxing in-home retreats, or repurpose existing spaces for new bedrooms or hobbies as the family dynamic changes throughout the years. We have the expertise, skills, and experience to design and construct a wide variety of large-scale interior renovation projects.

     

    Sagging Curb Appeal? Time for an Exterior Facelift

     

    Make your home the envy of the neighborhood with an update to the façade or exterior of your house. We can resurface outside walls, add brick or more windows, create country porches or dramatic stonework – all the options are available to you with Tabor. Our team of experienced craftsmen will make your home the standout property on the block. Discuss your project with a Tabor designer to get started!
