    Kitchen Remodeling

    Enhanced utility and style – the benefits of a kitchen remodel

     

    Imagine waking up each morning to be welcomed by a stunning space that is clean, polished and beautiful – the perfect way to start the day! As the kitchen is often a focal point of the home, boosting its visual appeal will produce dramatic effects on your entire space. A conscientiously-planned layout – including location of appliances, countertops, cabinets and other features – will enhance the food preparation and cleanup process itself, making things run smoother and more efficiently for the whole family. Updating appliances, water fixtures and lighting has the potential to increase resource efficiency, improving your home’s sustainability factor and potentially saving you money on energy and water bills. And should you decide to sell in the future, an updated modern kitchen will beckon potential buyers and can increase the value of your home overall.

    Kitchen Remodeling – The Way We Work

    The kitchen is the heart of the home. It’s a place for gathering, to make memories, and to safely and properly prepare the nourishment that fuels you and your family. Our goal is to make your dream kitchen a reality. We work closely with you to design, build and create a space that echoes your personal style, family’s needs and is efficiently functional. Many of our high-end kitchens feature top-of-the-line appliances, stunning granite countertops and intricate custom cabinetry – no matter how large or small the space. Whether you’re looking for a large center island to serve as a family meeting point or are a gourmet chef with a yearning for a pot filler and warming oven for breads, we can make it happen. From contemporary to traditional, cottage to upscale, art deco to retro, our award-winning team at Tabor Design Build will masterfully transform your kitchen into a showpiece that is as practical as it is beautiful.

       

    Customized Virtual-Reality Kitchen Design

     
     

    Using innovative 3D computerized software our custom kitchen remodeling experts will make your kitchen designs come to life – right on the 52-inch TV screen in our showroom. This allows you to select cabinets, fixtures and countertops, arranging and re-configuring them until you produce the perfect look and function, knowing exactly how your kitchen will look before the hammer hits the first nail. We help you to choose from a wide selection of top-quality fixtures and materials from leading manufacturers – all with a pricing that fits your budget.

     

    Stress-Free Kitchen Remodel

     

    We understand a kitchen remodel can greatly disrupt your daily routine. Our goal is to make the process as stress-free as possible. At Tabor Design Build we handle all aspects of the kitchen remodeling process – from design to build – and will save you the hassle of having to coordinate with different contractors. We take care of all the details, allowing you to sit back and enjoy watching your new kitchen come to life.
